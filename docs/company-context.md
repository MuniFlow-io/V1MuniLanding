# Muniflow - Company Context

## Company Overview
**Muniflow** (Muni Flow) is a bond deal workflow management platform that addresses critical pain points in the municipal bond industry.

## What We Do
We provide software solutions that streamline and optimize:
- **Legal work** in bond deals
- **Workflow management** for complex bond transactions
- **Communication** between stakeholders
- **Email coordination** and documentation
- Overall bond deal process efficiency

## Current State
- **Production App**: We have a fully functional app in production
- **User Base**: Active users who are interested in the platform
- **Credibility**: Strong enough traction that we have podcast interest
- **Market Position**: Early stage with proven product-market fit
