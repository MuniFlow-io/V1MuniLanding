# Passport: Homepage - Conversation First

## Page Overview
The **Homepage** is a calm introduction, credibility signal, and conversation starter. This is NOT a sales page, feature list, or product reveal.

**Mental Model**: "The first 5 minutes of a thoughtful conversation with someone who understands the municipal bond deal space."

## Purpose
- Let the right people recognize this is for their world
- Establish credibility through domain understanding
- Start conversations with qualified prospects
- Signal thoughtful, intentional building approach

## Tone & Voice
- **Quiet confidence** - We know this space
- **Professional** - Serious, not playful
- **Specific** - Municipal bond deals, not generic B2B
- **NO hype** - No "revolutionizing" or "disrupting"
- **NO urgency** - No pressure, no FOMO
- **NO volume optimization** - Quality conversations over traffic

## Design Direction

### Visual Approach
- **Clean, spacious layouts** - Generous white space
- **Professional typography** - Readable, serious
- **Minimal decoration** - No flashy animations or effects
- **Subtle depth** - Maybe glass cards on neutral backgrounds, not colorful
- **Calm colors** - Blues, grays, whites - professional, not playful

### Component Usage
- Use **Card** components (solid variant primarily)
- Use **Button** components (primary for CTAs, secondary for optional)
- Keep glass effects minimal or skip them - this isn't the demo page
- Focus on **typography and spacing** over visual effects
